FAQ

How do I remove FeedWind branding?
The Feedwind branding below your widgets will be automatically removed after we have confirmed your payment.
Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards.
Is the free plan really free?
Yes. With the FeedWind Free plan, you have access to all the standard features at no cost. Free widgets will have a backlink to FeedWind visible just below your widget.
Why should I upgrade to the Pro plan?
You will no longer have the FeedWind branding below the widget and you will be able to store 20 widgets (the Free plan allows for 5 widgets).
More questions?
If you have more questions or want to know more about FeedWind Pro, please check out the FeedWind Pro FAQ or contact us.
